What's Inside INTRA

INTRA centralizes NSW's operational knowledge into 12+ core categories, covering compliance, carriers, products, operations, and third-party relationships. This page details coverage areas, system capabilities, and the ongoing knowledge expansion roadmap.

System Capabilities

AI-Powered Retrieval

INTRA uses retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to surface verified answers from internal documentation. Unlike general-purpose AI, INTRA is built on NSW's specific knowledge base—carrier guides, compliance manuals, operations procedures, and regulatory updates.

✓
Contextual Understanding: INTRA interprets queries in context, understanding carrier names, product types, and regulatory references.
✓
Source Attribution: Answers include references to source documents, allowing verification and deeper research when needed.
✓
Continuous Updates: The knowledge base is refreshed quarterly with carrier updates and real-time regulatory changes.

What INTRA Can Answer

  • Carrier-specific underwriting requirements and application procedures
  • Product specifications, features, and comparisons
  • Account minimums and investment thresholds
  • Regulatory compliance standards and examination priorities
  • Operations workflows and standard procedures
  • Third-party relationships and vendor protocols
  • Tax treatment frameworks and planning considerations

What INTRA Cannot Answer

  • Client-specific advice or personalized recommendations
  • Real-time market data or investment performance
  • Complex legal or tax interpretations requiring professional judgment
  • Proprietary client information or case-specific details
  • Undocumented informal procedures or relationship nuances
For situations requiring judgment, interpretation, or client-specific analysis, consult your team or compliance officer.
